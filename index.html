CA HOI HOANG

We love music

Ca Hoi Hoang is a Vietnamese alternative rock band formed in 2013 by vocalist Thanh Luke (Nguyen Viet Thanh) and guitarist Nguyen Thanh Minh. In 2014, the band released their debut album titled Chapter II and added Bui Khac Dat and Le Dang Hieu to the group. In 2016, Ca Sal Hoang released a second album titled Paper Dream, followed by the double album Fold–Hurry: Does it need to make sense? respectively in 2017 and 2018. Also in 2017, Le Dang Hieu left the group to pursue his own artistic path. The current lineup of the group includes Thanh Luke (lead vocals), Thanh Minh (lead guitar, backing vocals, frontman) and Bui Khac Dat (bass guitar). The band's latest album, Escape Effect was released in 2019, along with F(x) cross-Vietnam tours through many big cities such as Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Da Nang, Hue. and Can Tho. Since its establishment until now, Ca Hoi Hoang has made a big splash in the indie/underground music community in Ho Chi Minh City in particular and the Vietnamese music market in general, and has received many publications about it. Vietnamese music is appreciated.
